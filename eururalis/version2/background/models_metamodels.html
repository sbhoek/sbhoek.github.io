The impact of the planet indicators is computed based on the output of the model chain. Some indicators only make use of the land use results (from CLUE) other indicators also use results from GTAP or IMAGE. The computations that result in the indicator impacts are registered in meta models. This is a new development implemented in Eururalis 2.0. A major advantage is that the recording of the procedure facilitates reproduction. This means that re-runs can be carried out by the Eururalis core team with no need for involvement of individual indicator specialists.

For each indicator a factsheet is available which gives information on the procedure of calculations and on the input data used to run the procedure.
