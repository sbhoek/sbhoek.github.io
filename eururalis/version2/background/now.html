Eururalis 1.0 was released in 2004 as a discussion tool to stimulate discussions on rural development in EU 25 during the Dutch chairmanship of the EU.

Eururalis was used on many occasions. The most important being the meeting
of EU directors for rural development in the Netherlands, November 2004. Eururalis 1.0 has been received enthusiastically by policymakers. It is also used for educational purposes and widely discussed amongst scientists.

During the development of Eururalis 2.0 the following presentation have been held:

• Presentation York November 2006
• Presentation SAG meeting Amsterdam, April 2006 and Models
• Presentation PAGmeeting Kopenhagen, April 2006
• Presentation Youngfarmers Mainz, March 2006
• Presentation usergroup, 9 March 2006
