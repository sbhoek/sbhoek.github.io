Eururalis is all about looking ahead. There are still many scientific challenges and policy questions left which in the third version of Eururalis will be adressed. The aim of Eururalis 3.0 will be to work in international consortia on scientific methods for the Eururalis framework. Therefore, if you are intersted in participating anad have a scientific module to contribute please contact the project leader. This will take place in close cooperation with EU and national policy makers in order to make the results relevant to issues which are most important to them.

In Eururalis 3.0 some new policy topics will be addressed. Meetings with the policy makers have showed that the implementation of the Water Framework Directive and policies on Nature and Green services are a priority. Also the further elaboration fo the bio-energy modules in Eururalis, especially the implementation of second generation bio-energy and the use of waste material is planned in Eururalis 3.0.

In addition to new policy options an indicator on greenhouse gasses is needed. Furthermore the regionalization of the economic models will bring further improvement to the overall model.
