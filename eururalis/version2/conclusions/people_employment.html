Highlights
Employment growth in the total economy is driven by population growth which is based on demographic trends. In general it is low in the EU25. In the EU15 it is slightly positive in the globalization scenarios and slightly negative in the regionalization scenarios. In the EU12 total employment growth is lower than in the EU15. In the regionalization scenarios employment growth declines with about 12% in the 2000-2030 period.
