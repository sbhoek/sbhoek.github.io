What is EURURALIS ?

EURURALIS offers facts and figures concerning the coming decades in 10 yrs time steps for the EU 25 rural area, based upon four contrasting scenarios and models predicting economical, ecological and socio-economic processes.

It offers three layers of information giving increasing detail:
1 headlines, maps, pictures;
2.an explanatory layer and
3.basic information.


EURURALIS integrates a variety of knowledge sources and modeling results in the socio-economic, enviromental and ecological domain. Changes in land use are at the heart.
