Why EURURALIS

Europe's rural areas will witness massive and rapid changes affecting Profit, People, Planet (3P). Major threats should be avoided and opportunities grasped. An anticipative and coordinated policy should be based upon facts, figures and insights in relationships. Balanced sustainable development is the compass to steer by.

Some examples of policy issues in rural areas:   

What are the effects of 10 new members of the EU on income or labour in agriculture in existing and new member states?

Can land use contribute to Kyoto goals?




Is extensive land use vanishing and what are the effects on nature?
