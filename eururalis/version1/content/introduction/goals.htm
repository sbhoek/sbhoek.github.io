What are the aims of EURURALIS ?

We present data and insights to fuel discussions on threats and opportunities and policy options. It's a discussion support rather than a decision support system.



What is the angle of view?

We approach future developments taking scenario-methods as entrance trying to depict possible effects for the three domains of sustainable development: ecology. economy, socio-cultural aspects (People, Planet, Profit). Effects are expressed in maps, graphs, pictures; various aspects can be combined and judged upon their contribution to sustainability. EURURALIS starts from global drivers, focuses on the EU25 and presents more detailed examples for representative regions.



For whom is EURURALIS ?

Three target groups can find their way :

  • policy makers
  • committed groups or individuals
  • colleagues in research
