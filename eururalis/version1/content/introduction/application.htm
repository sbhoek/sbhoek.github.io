What is EURURALIS ?

EURURALIS offers facts and figures concerning the coming decades for the EU 25 rural area, based upon four contrasting scenarios and models predicting economical, ecological and socio-economic processes. Central angle of view is how a three fold sustainability in ecological, economical and socio-cultural sense ( People, Planet, Profit approach) can be reached.


The application consists of a static and a dynamic part. At the moment you are in the static part and this part offers three layers of information giving increasing detail:


1 On the first level the main messages of the storyline are given, illustrated with maps and photos;


2. On the second level the main messages are explained and illustrated with examples;


3.The third level gives background information (pdf format).

 

The dynamic part provides the possibility to look and make maps and graphs of various indicators and compare them with indicator maps of different years and/or with different scenarios.
