Trends in Landuse in Mediterranean countries

Increase in urbanization (especially along coasts)

Increase of coastal tourism

Decrease of traditional agriculture

Increase of modern and irrigated agriculture


Trends in Landuse in Alpine regions

Increase in urbanization and traffic in valleys

Increase in skiing pistes

Decrease of glaciers

Decrease of traditional extensive farming at higher grounds


Trends in Landuse in Central and East European countries

Increase in urbanization; drain from rural areas

Increase in forested areas

Decrease of traditional agriculture

Transition from state owned farms to private farms


Trends in landuse in North-west Europe

Increase in urbanisation

Scale enlargement / intensification in farming

Decrease in nature and locally restoration

Increase in agro-industrial complexes


Trends in land use Northern Europe

Decrease extensive agriculture/ land abandonment

Increase nature-tourism

Some urbanisation
