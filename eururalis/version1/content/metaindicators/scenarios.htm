Scenario intentions compared to modeled outcomes give counterintuitive results


General expectations of world visions in A1, A2, B1 and B2 Scenarios have been confronted to the EURURALIS outcomes for People, Planet and Profit indicators that were output of our models The table below shows the outcomes where especially the anomalies between expectations and models are interesting.
We observed the following contradictory notions:


  • Food self sufficiency seems to be best provided by scenario A1 instead of the B2 priority;
  • Greenhouse mitigation will be much more supported by scenarios A1, B1 and B2 instead of A2;
  • Wealth convergence in the EU25 is not mainly supported by A2 and B2 scenarios. B2 scores, in fact the lowest of all scenarios;
  • Wealth convergence worldwide will profit evenly by A1, A2 and B1 scenarios.

The other prioritized intentions are more or less in line with the calculated results.
